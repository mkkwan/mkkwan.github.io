Introduction

Imi

Imi sells simple, quality, handmade jewelry. A minimal piece of jewelry can spice up an outfit and bring it to the next level. It shows class and let's the wearer express the world what's important to you! Thank you for giving our pieces a home.

Target audience

The target audience is trendy young adults and the the fashionable working class who want to spend a little to buy a treat for themselves. Generally, the audience will have social media and pay attention to keeping a sustainable and ethical lifestyle by supporting businesses with the same values. Anyone who wants to by a nice gift for a special someone can find what they're looking for at Imi and these gifts and special and unique.

Showcase meaningful jewelry and provide online experience for sales. Visit this site to browse our online collection and get the purchase delivered to their home. We want to bring customers in to the store as well so they can interact with the space and owners.
